Our History

Watikaya Charitable Organization is a community based organization fully registered with the Local Government of Uganda not for profit, Non-Governmental Organization.

Our Core Values

To reach the down-village people who are forgotten by top organisations and leadership but are suffering including Elderly, Children, Orphans, Widows and Widowers, refugees, Prisons, Mental Health people, People living with disabilities.
